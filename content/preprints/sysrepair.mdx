Autonomous vulnerability remediation must satisfy a dual objective: the patch must remediate
the vulnerability **and** the protected service must remain available. SysRepair evaluates
six solver architectures under a single protocol measuring accuracy, compute budget, and
whether an action sequence is reviewable before any change reaches the system. It introduces
**NeuroPlan**, a neural-symbolic solver that generates a PDDL domain and problem from each
scenario, validates it, and invokes a classical planner to produce a verifiable plan.
