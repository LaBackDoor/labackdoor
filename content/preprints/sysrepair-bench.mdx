A reproducible benchmark of 313 Dockerized and virtual-machine scenarios across six suites.
Each scenario is scored on two mandatory objectives — the vulnerability is no longer
exploitable, and all previously operational services remain functional — plus a
compensating-control objective for 39 scenarios where direct source patching is forbidden.
It contributes the Meta4 suite (modern CVEs: Log4Shell, Spring4Shell, PwnKit, Dirty Pipe,
the XZ backdoor, and more) and Hivestorm, a system-wide remediation track.
