A survey of the emerging field of autonomous vulnerability remediation, where agents reason
about system state, choose defensive actions, make changes, and verify that risk is reduced
without operational failures. Screening 470 records, it organizes the literature into six
streams and formalizes autonomous system repair as a closed-loop task model (observation,
diagnosis, action synthesis, safety gating, execution, verification, rollback, evidence).
