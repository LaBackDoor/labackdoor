This is the home of **la backdoor**, a security research lab focused on data-driven defenses:
threat detection and resilience for cyber-physical systems, AI security, and network
intelligence.

We will use this blog for research notes, paper write-ups, and project updates. Our
[publications](/publications) sync automatically from Google Scholar, our
[research](/research) page tracks ongoing work, and our code lives at
[github.com/LaBackDoor](https://github.com/LaBackDoor).

Look around — type `help` in the terminal on the home page, or use the navigation up top.
