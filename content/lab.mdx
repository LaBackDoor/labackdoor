la backdoor is a security research lab working on **data-driven solutions for threat
detection and resilience in cyber-physical systems**. We combine machine learning, NLP/LLMs,
and classical security analysis to build defenses that hold up against real adversaries.

## Research areas

- **Threat detection & CPS resilience** — data-driven detection and resilience for
  cyber-physical and networked systems.
- **AI security** — applying (and stress-testing) machine-learning and large language models
  for security, including byte-level models for network analysis.
- **Network & protocol intelligence** — deep packet inspection and protocol-level analysis
  driven by learned representations.
- **Security analytics** — forecasting software-vulnerability trends and mining security
  signal from large, noisy data sources.
