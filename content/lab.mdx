## What we do

We research the low level: malware internals, the security of machine-learning
systems, and intrusion detection.

### Research areas

- **Systems & malware** — reverse engineering, unpacking, behavioral analysis.
- **AI security** — adversarial ML, model extraction, data poisoning.
- **IDS** — detection engineering and sensor design.
