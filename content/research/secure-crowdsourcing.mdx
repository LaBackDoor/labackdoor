This project builds an NLP/language-model pipeline for **securely crowdsourcing firsthand
accounts from conflict zones**. Applied to ~250,000 Telegram messages from the
Russo-Ukrainian conflict, it surfaced 477 new contextual groupings across 13 news sources.

Published at **ASONAM 2024** (Springer LNCS, vol. 15212).
