Abanisenioluwa Kolawole Orojo is a Computer Science PhD researcher at **Baylor University**
(2023–2027) and the founder of la backdoor. His work centers on **data-driven threat
detection and resilience in cyber-physical systems**, spanning cloud security,
network-protocol security, security analytics, and AI security.

He earned his MS and BS in Computer Science at Webster University before beginning doctoral
research under Pablo Rivas and Erika Leal. His publications apply machine learning and
NLP/LLM techniques to security problems — from byte-level models for deep packet inspection
to recurrent-network forecasting of software-vulnerability trends and secure social-media
crowdsourcing.
