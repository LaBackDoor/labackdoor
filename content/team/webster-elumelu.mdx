Webster C. Elumelu is an External Collaborator and Researcher with la backdoor, affiliated
with 78 Financials. A Computer Science graduate of Webster University, he works at the
intersection of **cybersecurity and frontend engineering**, contributing to the lab's
research initiatives.

He co-authored **"Predicting Software Vulnerability Trends with Multi-Recurrent Neural
Networks"** (NLPAICS 2024, Lancaster, UK), which introduces multi-recurrent networks for
time-series forecasting of software-vulnerability trends.
